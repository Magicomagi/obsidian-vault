**1. Account Creation:**
- Access the NextDNS website (https://nextdns.io/) and establish an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button and entering your preferred credentials.

**2. iOS Configuration:**
- Download the "NextDNS" application from the App Store.
- On the NextDNS website, locate and copy the unique "ID" displayed within your account.
- Open the NextDNS app and tap the three dots at the bottom of the screen.
- Enable the "Custom Configuration" option and paste the previously copied "ID" into the designated field. Confirm your changes by tapping "Done."
- Activate the switch located in the center of the screen.
- A menu will appear. Click "Open Settings" and navigate to the "General" section.
- Scroll down and locate "VPN & Device Management."
- Select "DNS" and choose "NextDNS" from the available options.

**3. Android Configuration:**
- On the NextDNS website, locate and copy the "DNS-over-TLS/QUIC" address displayed within your account.
- Access the system settings application on your Android device.
- Navigate to "Network & internet" and then proceed to "Advanced."
- Select "Private DNS."
- Choose the "Private DNS provider hostname" option.
- Paste the previously copied "DNS-over-TLS/QUIC" address into the designated field and tap "Save."

**4. Implementing Parental Controls:**

- Return to the NextDNS website and navigate to the "Parental Controls" page.
- Utilize the "Add Category" function to select categories of content you wish to block.
- Enable "Safe Search" and "YouTube Restricted Mode" for additional filtering.
- On the "Denylist" page, you may add specific websites that you desire to restrict access to.
