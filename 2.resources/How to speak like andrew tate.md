Do you remember when you watched your FIRST ever self-improvement video? The essence of the video basically talks about replacing the BAD habits with GOOD ones.

And what about it? The same thing applies to Filler words. You don’t suddenly quit using filler words, you replace it. What you replace it with is the “PAUSE”.

YOU + FILLER WORDS = INCOMPETENT

YOU - FILLER WORDS + PAUSES = A MASTER OF STORY SELLING

Speak more. No kidding. The reason why Andrew Tate is SO good at speaking… To no one’s surprise; It’s his job. Hamza? Same rule applies. Both men speak to their audience in an engaging, vigorous way which ALLOWS THEM to persuade us, you, the audience.

The upcoming phase will be the most important. To enter the next phase however, I will need your full presence, attention, and your mind occupied with what I am about to tell you. Follow the actionable steps that I laid out for YOU.

Actionable Steps🛠️📈

1st KEY to Power - When someone asks you a question, pause for a moment and think about it. Then answer with a chill and relaxed tone. This will give the person you’re interacting with the idea that you are a well-thought-out man. Additionally, you will become more respected by everyone because you actually give out answers that come from within you, not some impulses to please people.

- E.g. Whenever I would be called upon by my teachers, asked by my friends, I would take a pause and the room would go silent. 1-3 seconds later, I will respond, and this signifies power and control. I became more respected. IF someone interrupts you in the midst of the silence, it signifies that you need to keep working on yourself. Remember, no one interrupts the king.

2nd KEY to Presence - Eye contact is key for this section. Every time you’re interacting with someone, maintain eye contact. However, you shouldn’t stare at them as it would be weird in a way. Instead, look away for a moment, and then refocus on the person’s eyes. Exactly like meditation! Keep in mind, you need to be active in interactions, remember that people want to talk about themselves more than they want to hear you talk about you. To take advantage of this, be an active listener and ask questions.

This way, you get to satisfy the person’s desire to be heard, but you also learn. In addition, I would want to talk about verbal communications in this post but that would make this way too long, therefore a quick piece of advice for you in the body language realm is to maintain a confident, and open body language.

- E.g. When walking down the street, hallways, etc., maintain a good body posture. Chest out and shoulders back. Always walk with purpose.

3rd KEY to Speaking - We’ve already talked about filler words and replacing them with pauses. But the actionable step for this one is to simply enjoy social interactions. Everything else that I’ve mentioned will grow futile unless you tackle communication with others as fun. The point most of the time in a social setting is to have this sense of fulfilment.

- E.g. When interacting with others, always have this in mind; The goal is to have fun. There’s no pressure to make jokes and what not, but don’t always be serious when talking to others. There are times to be serious and to be relaxed.

4th KEY to Story Selling - Talk to more people. I challenge you to talk to a stranger every day for 30 days. This will sound intimidating at first but don’t be. This can be as quick as a compliment to a random person you passed by. It’s quick and easy, and if you don’t, you’re missing out on potential friends, or even a romantic relationship.
