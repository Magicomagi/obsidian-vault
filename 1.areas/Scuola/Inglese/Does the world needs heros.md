find 2 arguments to support (does the world need the heros) and go against it
## Support
1. **Heroes bring us together:** When bad things happen, heroes can unite us. They remind us that we're not alone and give us hope for the future
2. **Example to follow:** A hero can be a figure that can be follow to fix the problem

## Against
1. **Heroes can distract from the real solutions:** We might focus on celebrating the hero instead of fixing the underlying issues that caused the problem in the first place.
2. **individual responsability:** When there is a hero we think that the resposnsability goes all to the hero
